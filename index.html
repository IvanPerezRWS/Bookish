About Bookish

When you buy a book, statistically speaking, you are only going to read the book once. So why not give an opportunity for other people to read the books you enjoyed. Bookish is a literature focused social media platform that allows users to message eachother to trade books. Bokish is an app where you can connect with many book lovers from all over the world.
